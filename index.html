Risk Metrics Analysis

Sharpe Ratio

Risk-adjusted return metric accounting for volatility. Higher values indicate better risk-adjusted performance.

Best: PPO (1.85)

Worst: MVO (0.92)

Sortino Ratio

Similar to Sharpe but considers only downside volatility. Better for asymmetric return distributions.

Best: PPO (2.31)

Worst: MVO (1.15)

Maximum Drawdown

Largest peak-to-trough decline. Lower (more negative) values indicate higher risk.

Best: MVO (-8.2%)

Worst: DDPG (-22.5%)

Algorithm Characteristics

PPO - Proximal Policy Optimization

Type: On-policy Actor-Critic

Key Feature: Clipped surrogate objective prevents large policy updates

Best For: Stable training with good sample efficiency

Performance: Best overall performer (27.14% return)

A2C - Advantage Actor-Critic

Type: On-policy Actor-Critic

Key Feature: Synchronous advantage estimation

Best For: Fast convergence with moderate stability

Performance: Second best (24.21% return)

DDPG - Deep Deterministic Policy Gradient

Type: Off-policy Actor-Critic

Key Feature: Deterministic policy with experience replay

Best For: Continuous action spaces

Performance: Third place (18.52% return)

TD3 - Twin Delayed DDPG

Type: Off-policy Actor-Critic

Key Feature: Double Q-learning with delayed policy updates

Best For: Reducing overestimation bias

Performance: Moderate (15.73% return)

SAC - Soft Actor-Critic

Type: Off-policy Actor-Critic with entropy regularization

Key Feature: Maximum entropy framework encourages exploration

Best For: Robust policies in stochastic environments

Performance: Lower tier (12.94% return)

MVO - Mean-Variance Optimization

Type: Traditional portfolio optimization (baseline)

Key Feature: Markowitz efficient frontier

Best For: Risk-averse strategies

Performance: Conservative baseline (9.33% return)

Limitations & Future Research

Current Limitations

  • Hourly data may miss intraday volatility patterns
  • Transaction costs simplified (no slippage modeling)
  • No consideration of market impact for large orders
  • Training limited to 21-month period
  • No multi-asset correlation modeling

Future Directions

  • Incorporate sentiment analysis from news/social media
  • Test on multiple market conditions (bull/bear/sideways)
  • Ensemble methods combining multiple algorithms
  • Real-time deployment with paper trading validation
  • Transfer learning across different stock exchanges
